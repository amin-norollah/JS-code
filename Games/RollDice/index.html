The game roles

Each player should roll dice by pressing the "Roll dice" button. If the dice number is between 2 and 6, it is added to the "current score". The player can collect his points by pressing the "Hold" button and giving the turn to the opponent.

If the dice become number 1, the player's high score is reduced by the current score.

The player wins if his score is more than 50 points.
